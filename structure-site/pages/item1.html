sad sandwich

The sandwich had chicken in the name. There was no chicken, or much of anything in this sandwich. Just a sad flavored baguette. As if a greedy goblin went in my sandwich and took everything inside!! At least I know I can only go up from this sandwich experience, and I've had better sandwiches since this sad day.
