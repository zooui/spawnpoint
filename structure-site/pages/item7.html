a "W" dropcap

My favorite dropcap style letter I made as part of a group project. We made our typeface capital letters very illustrative and in theme with the Red Queen from Alice in Wonderland(2010). It was fun but exhaustive, I find myself procrastinating these kinds of things but then can't put the pencil down once I get going. With this capital "W" I was thinking about the wings of the Jabberwocky from the movie.
